Frequently Asked Questions

Is WorkBrew good for long study sessions?

Yes. We're designed for 6–12 hour sessions with ergonomic chairs, power at every seat, and no minimum order pressure.

Do you offer student discounts in Hyderabad?

Flat 20% off on all food and beverages with a valid student ID, plus dedicated student membership plans.

How fast is the WiFi?

1Gbps symmetric with dual-ISP failover — fast enough for HD video calls and large model downloads.

Can I book a slot in advance?

Yes, head to our booking dashboard to reserve quiet zones up to 7 days out.
